DIE HART

Kevin Hart, playing a version of himself, sets out to pivot from being pigeon-holed as a comedy wingman to being taken seriously as an action movie star. Hart attends ‘action hero school’ run by Travolta’s character where he attempts to learn the ropes on how to become one of the industry’s most coveted action stars.

Actors:Kevin Hart, Nathalie Emmanuel

Director:Eric Appel
